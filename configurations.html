The Module System

One Triangle, Infinite Configurations

Every Atlan module is an 858mm (34") equilateral triangle. Two triangles bolt together to form a square. Squares connect to build any size or shape you need.

1 Module

858mm triangle. The building block of every island.

1 Two-Pack

Two triangles form a ~34" square. Approximately 8 sq ft. $518 per 2-pack.

Connect & Expand

Nylon bolts join squares into any layout. Add modules anytime.
